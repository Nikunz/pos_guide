            <h2>Steps to Use GoDaddy POS</h2>
            <ol>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 1: Set Up Your Account</h3>
                    <p>Visit the GoDaddy website and create an account. Choose the POS system plan that fits your business needs.</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 2: Download the GoDaddy POS App</h3>
                    <p>Download the GoDaddy POS app from the App Store or Google Play Store on your device.</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 3: Connect Your Hardware</h3>
                    <p>Connect your card reader, barcode scanner, and other necessary hardware to your device as per the instructions provided.</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 4: Configure Your Settings</h3>
                    <p>Log in to the app and configure your settings, including payment methods, tax rates, and inventory.</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 5: Add Products and Services</h3>
                    <p>Add your products and services to the system, ensuring to include prices and descriptions.</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 6: Process Transactions</h3>
                    <p>To process a sale, select the items being purchased, accept payment, and send receipts to your customers.</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h3>Step 7: Review Sales Reports</h3>
                    <p>Use the reporting features to review sales data, track inventory levels, and analyze your business performance.</p>
                </li>
            </ol>
        </section>
